Inside Our Upperhill Office Transformation 🏢

Posted on: July 11, 2025

Hybrid Interiors recently completed a stunning commercial redesign in Upperhill for a prominent insurance company. The space, once a dull and uninspiring office block, is now a sleek, collaborative, and energizing work environment tailored to boost productivity and brand identity.

💼 Project Brief

The client wanted an office that reflects trust, innovation, and professionalism — values core to their business. We reimagined the entire floor plan, integrating:

  • Open-plan workspaces with acoustic ceiling panels
  • Private executive suites and boardrooms
  • Modern reception with brand-colored LED lighting
  • Green breakout spaces with custom planters

🧱 Materials & Finish

To reflect the company’s premium positioning, we used high-quality materials:

  • Matte black aluminum partitions
  • Oakwood laminate flooring
  • Custom acrylic wall branding
  • Anti-glare task lighting systems
